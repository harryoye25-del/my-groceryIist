📦 Freeze extra shrimp, salmon and bread immediately when you get home.
❄️
Freeze on arrival: 🐟 Fish fillets — portion into 150–200g pieces, wrap tightly, freeze. Thaw in fridge overnight. 🍤 Shrimp — freeze in original bag; thaw under cold running water in 5 min. 🍞 Bread — freeze one loaf right away; toast straight from frozen. 🥑 Avocados — buy a mix of ripe (eat in 1–2 days) and firm (ready by Wed–Thu).
